Candidate Terms & Conditions

1. Scope of Services

• Free Registration: Staffnest Solutions provides comprehensive recruitment and placement assistance to Candidates with free registration upfront.

• No Guarantee: The Agency does not guarantee definite employment, guaranteed interviews, or final placement within any explicit timeframe.

• Matching Rights: The Agency reserves the sole right to filter and determine which job profiles accurately match the Candidate's background skills.

• Training: Fresher candidates may be provided with basic hospitality training completely free of cost to boost interface skills and baseline employability.

2. Candidate Obligations

• Accurate Information: The Candidate must provide true, complete, and updated statements regarding qualifications, experience data, and background history.

• Verification: The Agency or hiring employers may perform deep background checks, reference checks, or educational credential verification at any stage.

• Attendance: The Candidate must attend scheduled interviews punctually and contact the Agency immediately if an emergency cancellation arises.

3. Interview and Selection Process

• Direct Communication: Candidates must not make unprompted direct contact with prospective corporate employers unless explicitly authorized by us.

• Feedback Loop: The Candidate agrees to provide prompt post-interview feedback to the Agency immediately after completion.

• Offers Tracker: The Candidate must inform the Agency immediately upon receiving an official job offer from an employer we referred.

4. Confidentiality & Data Privacy

• Non-Disclosure: Information regarding employers, open placement specs, and internal interview patterns must be held in strict confidence.

• Data Handling: The Agency manages personal information safely according to standard rules and forwards profiles only to targeted employers.

5. Liability & Financial Commitments

• Operational Disclaimers: Staffnest Solutions is not responsible for physical contract breaches, internal salary disputes, or disciplinary actions handling post-hire.

• Service Fee: Placement assistance is completely free initially. However, the candidate agrees that a one-time specified placement service fee will be automatically deducted from their first month's salary by the employer and remitted directly to the Agency.

• Enforcement: In cases of intentional evasion or non-payment of the agreed remittance fee, Staffnest Solutions will pursue formal legal recovery operations under the exclusive jurisdiction of the Siliguri courts only.
